What the restaurant assessment looked for.

Focused Map

A three-hour read on one operational pain point, a focused operating window, short owner/staff interviews, a written assessment, and a review meeting.

Broad Map

A deeper look at labor, food cost, waste, receiving, shift handoffs, and audit readiness when something is leaking and the operator is not sure where.

  • A map of how work moves through the store, including handoffs and places work falls through.
  • Top repetitive failure points, rough cost, and no-cost or low-cost fixes.
  • An honest fit call on what should stay human-owned and where a support workflow could safely help.
