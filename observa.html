Observa · Evidence integrity for AI

Your AI agent acted. Can you prove what happened?

Observa helps teams preserve the evidence trail after AI workflows run — so owners can reconstruct what happened, what was approved, what is unknown, and what needs review.

Uptime tells you whether the system was reachable. Evidence integrity tells you whether the system can be held accountable.

The problem

Runtime health is not accountability.

Most tools can tell you whether a system is online, whether a workflow ran, or whether an agent completed a task. But once AI agents start sending, changing, booking, updating, or recommending, the harder question is whether the record is strong enough to explain what happened later.

Uptime is not evidence integrity.

A system can be reachable while the evidence path underneath it degrades. Observa focuses on the accountability layer: records, approvals, gaps, confidence levels, follow-ups, and owner review.

What Observa tracks

The pieces that make a record trustworthy.

Known facts

What the records actually support, stated plainly.

Unknown gaps

What the record can't answer — marked, not glossed over.

Evidence IDs

Every finding traces back to a specific record.

Approval trails

What was reviewed and approved before it acted.

Confidence levels

How certain each finding is — high vs. unconfirmed.

Evidence-path degradation

When the record itself becomes unreliable.

Follow-up items

What needs attention, surfaced from the activity.

Human review points

Where a person should look before it's closed.
