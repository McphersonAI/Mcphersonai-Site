# 14_known_limits — Known Limits

## What belongs here

The known-limits snapshot for this pilot: what the deployment does not cover, module-specific limits, and operator-communicated limitations.

## What must NOT be stored here

Limits discovered mid-pilot left undocumented — new limits get added here and raised at the weekly proof review.

## Reminder

No secrets, no `.env` files, and no real client data in any folder that could reach GitHub. Real pilot folders stay local/private.
