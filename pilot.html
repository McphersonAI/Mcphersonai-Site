Labor Variance Tracking
Daily labor-vs-revenue tracking with mid-week alerts. Hours-to-cut math runs before payroll closes, not after.
Food Cost Diagnostics

How It Works

Each store gets its own private system. Your data stays scoped to your store. No shared infrastructure with other clients. No cross-contamination between deployments.

Your team interacts through Telegram on their phones. Natural language in, structured records out. The agent listens, echoes back what it understood, and writes only after explicit confirmation.

Setup is included. McPherson AI handles deployment, configuration, and onboarding. Your team is using the system the same week.

Founder pilots are about getting the first deployments right. Three operators, flat pricing, full setup, direct access to the founder. The goal is to refine the system in real operating environments — not to sell as much as possible as fast as possible.
