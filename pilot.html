What the Diagnostic Produces

The goal is not a generic AI demo. The goal is a clear operator assessment: what is breaking, what should stay human-owned, and what could be safely supported in a pilot.

1
Current Workflow Map
Visualize how work currently moves through the store, including handoffs and communication points.
2
Top Repetitive Failure Points
Identify recurring breakdowns that cause friction, delays, mistakes, or rework.
3
What Should Stay Human-Owned
Clarify the decisions and responsibilities that should remain with the team.
4
What Could Be Assisted Safely
Show the tasks an AI assistant can support in the background to save time and reduce errors.
5
Required Store Context / Memory
Capture the repeating context, habits, policies, and local knowledge the system would need to remember.
6
Recommended Pilot Scope
Define one focused starting point with a clear plan and measurable outcome.
